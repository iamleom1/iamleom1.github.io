Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Overview

R4V3 is a mobile application for discovering events, RSVPing, finding rave crews, and chatting with matched users.

Data we collect

  • Account data: email address and authentication identifiers
  • Profile data: display name, age or birthdate, city, bio, vibe tags, genre preferences, and optional profile fields
  • Event activity: RSVPs, crew visibility state, matching actions, conversation activity, and moderation events
  • Device and permissions data: optional location data when you grant location permission and profile photos you choose to upload

How we use data

  • operate the app and user accounts
  • show nearby and event-based community matching
  • enable event RSVP and chat features
  • moderate safety reports, abuse, and policy violations
  • improve product performance and reliability

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with infrastructure providers needed to run the service, including authentication, database, storage, and event data providers.

Location data

Location is used to support nearby and event-based matching. Users can deny location access, and some matching functionality may be reduced when location is unavailable.

Photos and user content

Profile photos, messages, and reports are stored to provide the service and enforce safety policies.

Account deletion

Users can delete their account in-app from the Profile screen. Support can also assist with account deletion requests.
